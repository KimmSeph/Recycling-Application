Welcome to DecodeWaste

DecodeWaste is a smart, eco-friendly tool designed to help users make better waste disposal choices. By simply scanning a product's barcode or QR code, DecodeWaste instantly identifies whether the item is plastic, non-biodegradable, or recyclable.

This application empowers individuals and communities to reduce pollution, sort waste responsibly, and take a step toward a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
